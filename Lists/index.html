How to Boil an Egg

Directions to Boil an Egg

  1. Get an egg.
  2. Choose a pot.
  3. Add water to cover the eggs.
  4. Heat the water until it boils.
  5. Reduce the heat to simmer.
  6. Set a timer for 9-12 minutes.
  7. Cool the eggs in cold water and ice.
  8. Peel the eggs.
  9. Enjoy!
